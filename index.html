MY SAFE
SPOT.

I just like lying down with a favorite Netflix show on, playing a simple arcade game on my phone. That's it. That's my safe spot — my way to switch off and relax.

But I'm tired of an ad popping up every ten seconds. Clear a stage — ad. Fail a stage — ad. Get punished either way for the crime of… playing a game.

So I'm making free games instead. Not to shut down the games industry — I love games. I'm setting a baseline. Now that AI can build a polished casual game in a week, the bottom line of what a game should be just moved up. These are proof of that line.

Let's make free entertainment for everyone to enjoy. Insert no coin.

> — the person who got tired of being ad-punished_

THE MANIFESTO

Open-source, ad-free, free-forever casual games. Every game ships to Android and iOS at no cost — no ads, no paywalls, no data harvesting. Built with Flutter + Flame, all code public to inspect, fork, and improve.

WHAT THIS IS NOT

This is not an attack on developers. Developer talent is too valuable to spend ad-cloning games that already exist. Building something genuinely new and complex? Charge whatever you want. This movement is only about the simple arcade and puzzle games that require no ongoing creative labor — the ones that should never have been ad-farms in the first place.
